==Ingredients==
* 1 cup flour
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 1 cup (250ml) milk
* 2 tablespoons oil
* 2 teaspoons vanilla
* 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

==Procedure==

1. Preheat oven and oil an eight-inch oven-safe skillet.

2. Combine dry ingredients: flour, baking powder,
   baking soda, salt, and sugar.

3. Add the milk, oil, and vanilla; stir well.

4. Stir in the apple cider vinegar, mix well, and pour
   immediately into the skillet.

5. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes, and serve.

==Notes==

When a toothpick is inserted into the completed product, it
should come out clean.
